What is Study Buddy?

Study Buddy is a production tool web application comprising of a to-do list and a calendar to help you keep up with all your work.

What do we do?

Provide a efficient and effective calendar and reminder service, along with an incentive to keep you working

How it works

You pay a monthly fee with a minimum deposit of $5CAD. From there you set up a to-do list containing important tasks you want to complete like Homework, School Projects, Club meetings etc.


If all your tasks are completed by their deadlines, then you will recieve a full refund into your bank account.


"What happens if my tasks aren't completed by the deadline?"

If you do not complete a task within your certain deadline, then your money will be used to continue hosting this application to keep providing you with a seamless experience.
